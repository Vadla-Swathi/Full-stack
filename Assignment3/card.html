Munnar

Munnar is a hill station and town located in the Idukki district of Kerala, India. It's nestled amidst the Western Ghats mountain range, known for its stunning natural beauty, sprawling tea plantations, and cool climate. Munnar was once a resort for the British Raj elite. Today, it's a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors with its scenic landscapes, trekking trails, waterfalls, and rich biodiversity.Explore the Munnar tea plantations,Eravikulam National Park.
